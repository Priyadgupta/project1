Welcome to Fun and Learn

Fun and Learn Early Learning Centre is family owned and operated, long day care centre, located in Wentworthville/Cumberland Council. Open since 2022, this centre is purpose built and provide a homely nurturing environment for children.

Highlights:

  • Safe & Secure
  • Developmentally Appropriate Curriculum
  • Friendly, Caring, and Experienced Staff
  • First Aid and CPR certified
  • Hours of Operation: Monday-Friday from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
